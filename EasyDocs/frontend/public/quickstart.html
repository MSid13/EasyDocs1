Getting Started

  1. Sign Up or Log In: Start here! Use your username and password. If you‚Äôre new, sign up for a free account.
  2. Create or Open a Document: Click a document to open, or use the New Document button to start fresh.
  3. Edit & Collaborate: Type in the editor. Changes are synced in real time with collaborators. You can see others‚Äô edits instantly.
  4. Manual & Autosave: Click Save to save, or enable Autosave (toggle in the editor) for automatic saving every second.
  5. Share & Permissions: Owners can share documents with others and set their role (editor/viewer). Editors can edit, viewers can only view.
  6. Settings: Click Settings (top right) to change your password or switch between light/dark mode.
  7. Print: Use the Print button in the editor to print or export your document.
Tips for Success:
  • Use Autosave for peace of mind‚Äîno lost work!
  • Share documents only with trusted users. You control who can edit or view.
  • Change your password regularly for better security.
  • Admins can reset passwords and manage users in the admin panel.
  • If you see connection or sync issues, check your internet and make sure the Yjs server is running.
  • For best experience, use a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari).

Security & Privacy

  • Your password is always encrypted (bcrypt) and never stored in plain text.
  • Only authenticated users can access or edit documents.
  • Your admins can help you recover your account if you forget your password.
  • All document changes are synced securely over WebSockets.
